French Fries

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Oil
  • Cold Water
  • Rosemary
  • Salt
  • Red Chilli Powder

  1. Wash ands Peel the Skin of potatoes.
  2. Cut them into 1/2 inch thick slice and then into thin strips of uniform size
  3. Wash the strips in plain water or ice water for 2-3 times to nremove the excess starch content
  4. Boil 2 cups of water in a pan.
  5. Switch off the flame and soak the potato strips in it for 5 minutes. No need to cover the bowl.
    6. Drain the water completely.
